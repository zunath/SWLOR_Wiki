Content and World

  • Korriban has been updated.
  • A new Hunter's Guild task has been added.
  • New appearance parts and heads are now available.
  • New lightsaber colors have been added: Green, Purple, Cyan, Violet, and Brown.

Combat and Abilities

  • The DC for Knockdown has been adjusted to 15.
  • Damage for Circle Slash and Double Strike has been increased.
  • Circle Slash now affects up to six targets instead of three.
  • Force Drain now has a maximum distance of 20 meters.
  • Wrist Rocket now takes 0.5 seconds to activate.
  • Wrist Rocket's knockdown effect now lasts 2 seconds.
  • Wrist Rocket now only targets hostile targets.
  • Infusion I now heals 60 HP per tick, up from 20 HP per tick.
  • Infusion II now heals 120 HP per tick, up from 40 HP per tick.
  • Shocking Shout's recast delay is now 120 seconds, down from 180 seconds.
  • The calculation for Shocking Shout has been changed to rely more heavily on the Social attribute.
  • Provoke I and II no longer affect associates, including beasts and droids.
  • Steady Hand, Muscle Memory, and Veneration now grant the passive bonuses previously granted by attributes.
  • Heavy armor casting time penalties have been removed.
  • Adjusted the way tranquilizer works to hopefully fix the sleep effect once and for all. The tier 3 cone calculation was also modified to make it more consistent.

Crafting, Economy, and Items

  • Crafting is no longer dependent on attributes.
  • Improved Power Supply I-V now grants an additional Module Bonus point. Existing items will not be updated, so reach out to a DM if you need a replacement.
  • Guild tasks now pay more credits on successful job completion.
  • One-shot quests now pay 50% more credits on average.
  • Gems, Zinsiam, and similar items now sell for more credits to NPCs.
  • Wild bug parts now sell for fewer credits to NPCs.
  • Crafted items now sell for more credits. Higher quality crafted items also sell to NPCs for more credits.
  • Fixed mark up and mark down on Adega's store.

Systems and Quality of Life

  • Characters on the same account can no longer purchase items via the market from each other.
  • In the event of a tie, the incumbent mayor will be the winner of an election, as originally intended.
  • The /stuck command has been improved to target a safe location in every area. This also works in DM areas, such as prefabs, where the command will send you back to CZ-220.
  • Due to changes with the /stuck command making it more reliable, its cooldown is now 30 minutes, up from 5 minutes.

Fixes

  • Fixed an issue where x99 stacks of Peeled Crayfish were not being recognized as bait.
  • Fixed an issue where Force Inspiration did not update the character sheet stats automatically.
  • Addressed several issues with Auras not applying or not getting removed at the appropriate times.
  • Fixed an issue where damage for non-Might based beasts was incorrect.
  • Fixed an issue where clamping damage based on the tier of weapon was not correctly applying.
  • Fixed an issue where "Grant" Permissions were not cleared during boot time.
