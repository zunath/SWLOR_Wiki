Changes

  • Stinky Womprat quest has been fixed. You may need to turn in one more womprat hide even if the quest was already complete before this update.
  • Fixed an issue with beast HP calculations being undone by a negative VIT modifier.
  • Fixed an issue where pet food destroyed the entire stack instead of one item.
  • Fixed an issue where Concussion Grenade II was not applying PER bonuses properly.
  • Adrenal Stim perk has been added.
  • Dantooine is now available.
  • A bug with Benevolence permanently applying has been fixed.
  • Benevolence recovery has been adjusted.
  • Premonition concealment has been adjusted.
  • Fixed an issue where buff timers did not display hours.
  • Research has been added, including a new Research Terminal which can only be placed inside Labs.
  • Crafting menus have been updated to support researched blueprints.
  • Superior Weapon Focus and Increased Multiplier may now be taken by Force Sensitives.
  • Texture improvements have been made to certain tilesets.
  • New helmets, armor, and heads have been added.
  • Anti-spawn triggers have been added to several gates in space.
  • Ewoks are now a playable race.
  • Reward now restores beast HP more effectively.
  • The space revamp is now live.
