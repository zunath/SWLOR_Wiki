Release Date 2024-04-18
NWSync Hash e383b5a092cf27760a9aa2a47397f0c29758f3fa

Contributors: Cyberwitch; Dafena The Ripping Force; Scorchys; QM Grumpy Hermit; Nightwolf Starfish.

Changes

  • Incubation perks have been added to Beast Mastery.
  • The Stabling perk has been refunded to all characters due to pricing changes.
  • The Stabling perk now has five levels, bringing the total number of storable beasts up to 6 when maxed.
  • A new Lab building type has been added to player cities.
  • A new Incubator crafting device has been added. These may only be placed within Labs in player cities.
  • New recipes have been added to Fabrication.
  • Might now reduces the chance of receiving a critical hit instead of Agility.
  • Perception now improves the chance of dealing a critical hit for all weapon types rather than just melee.
  • Leg Sweep, Slam, Force Leap, Saber Strike, Crippling Shot, Double Shot, Explosive Toss, Piercing Toss, Quick Draw, Circle Slash, and Double Strike now account for Strong Style, Zen Marksmanship, Flurry Style, Crushing Mastery, and Crushing Style when calculating damage.
  • Planet graphics have been upgraded.
  • Categories for incubation items have been added to the market menu.
  • After fully looting beasts, they leave behind an object that can be used to extract DNA for Incubation.
  • Mechanic Helmet has been added to the Smithery recipe list.
  • The CP bonus on Devotion Helmet has been fixed. Speak with a DM if you need a replacement.
  • The HP bonus on the Valor Cloak has been corrected.
  • Disturbance and Force Spark descriptions have been corrected.
  • The perk requirement for crafting Chiro Staff has been corrected.
  • Fixed the location of the Veles Residential taxi location.
  • Fixed an issue where the /follow command could jump players between areas.
  • Holographic creatures in CZ-220 are now immortal.
  • Hull Repairer and Shield Repairer modules now mention that they can be used on the user's ship too.
  • DSHL-002 recipe now creates the correct item.
  • Added a settings section for customizing the text color of languages, emotes, and OOC text.
  • The armor appearance editor has been updated to support per-part coloring.
  • The targeting icon for starships has been updated.
  • Various areas in Dathomir have been updated.
  • New recipes have been added to Agriculture.
  • Accessories and flowers are now on sale in Veles.
  • You can now change your sound set from the new Customize menu on the character sheet.
  • Custom portraits can now be entered into the portrait editor.
