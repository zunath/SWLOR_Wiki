2022-12-28 Release Notes

Back to Release Notes

Release Date 2022-12-28
NWSync Hash 8d745f1eb8653bca6b7260926bd86118d833185d

Contributors: Valo; Whyleth; Scorchys; Sytic.

Part 1

  • Fixed an issue causing the server restart timer to spam all players if a DM possessed an NPC.
  • Brought a floating skull back to the ground on Dathomir.
  • Fixed an issue where Rousing Shout II and III were not applying the proper healing.
  • Fixed an issue where aura AOEs would not be applied to brand new characters.
  • Fixed an issue where AOE abilities like Kolto Recovery, Premonition, and Battle Insight were not correctly applying to nearby party members.
  • Fixed an issue where +ACC gear was giving double the intended amount.
  • Increased the drop rates of high-quality leather and high-quality fiberplast from Kwi enemies on Dathomir.
  • Wild blood can now be stacked.
  • Clarified the amount of HP restored by the Med Kit perk line.
  • Leg Sweep, Crescent Moon, and Hacking Blade now target the user by default.
  • Fixed a rare crash issue involving an ability.
  • Fixed an issue where fishing points had more uses than expected.
  • The STM cost of Med Kit II-V has been reduced.
  • The WIL scaling on Med Kit I-V has been doubled.
  • The recast time of Benevolence has been reduced from 10 seconds to 8 seconds.
  • The First Aid skill level requirement for Resuscitation I has been reduced from 20 to 15.
  • The HP recovered by Resuscitation II and III has been adjusted to scale with the caster's WIL stat.
  • Infusion I and Infusion II have been added to First Aid. They restore 20 HP and 40 HP respectively every six seconds for 24 seconds, scaling with WIL.
  • Stats have been adjusted on various NPCs.
  • Creeping Terror now deals increased damage and applies damage immediately on hit.
  • Alacrity and Clarity perks have been added to the Shields perk line. They restore 4 FP and 4 STM respectively when an attack is blocked with a shield.

Part 2

  • Saving throws for Reflex, Will, and Fortitude have been added back from the base game.
  • MGT increases Fortitude by 1 point per modifier.
  • WIL increases Will by 1 point per modifier.
  • PER increases Reflex by 1 point per modifier.
  • Resistance checks on Force abilities have been adjusted to use these stats.
  • Ability effects now use saving throws instead of random percentage chances to apply. For example, Knockdown's 60% chance is now a DC 12 Fortitude check.
  • Saving throws have been added to the character sheet.
  • Corrected the Flashbang Grenade description to indicate accuracy and display the proper reduction.
  • All levels of Force Push now work on any target, with increasing DCs for higher tiers. Knockdown base length has been reduced from 6 seconds to 2 seconds and continues to scale by 0.5 seconds per WIL modifier.
  • Fixed an issue where Mind Trick could be used on Droid characters.
  • Shocking Shout base length has been reduced from 6 seconds to 2 seconds and now scales by 0.5 seconds per SOC modifier.
  • Crippling Shot's bind effect duration has been reduced from 12 seconds to 6 seconds.
  • Fixed an issue where Ion Grenade's stun effect would not apply to Droid characters.
  • Wrist Rocket's knockdown length has been reduced from 6 seconds to 3 seconds.
  • Concussion Grenade's knockdown length has been reduced from 6 and 8 seconds to a static 3 seconds.
  • Shield Resistance I and II have been added to the Shield perk line. These increase Will, Fortitude, and Reflex saving throws while equipped with a shield.
  • Creatures afflicted by Daze, Knockdown, Stun, and Tranquilize now receive immunity to that same effect for 20 seconds after the effect ends.

Part 3

  • Lightsaber and Saberstaff Upgrade Kit recipes have been added to Dathomir and Korriban loot tables.
  • Passion fruit can now be stacked.
  • Fixed an issue with a number of Fabrication recipes missing components.
