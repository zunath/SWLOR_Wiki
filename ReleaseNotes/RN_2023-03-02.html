Changes

  • The Beast Mastery skill has been added.
  • Perks have been added to Beast Mastery. These primarily scale off the Social stat of the Beastmaster.
  • 21+ perks have been added for beasts. Stat scaling depends on the ability and uses 50% of the Beastmaster's stats and 50% of the beast's stats.
  • Provoke now scales off VIT instead of SOC.
  • Beast perks have been added to the perk menu.
  • Your active beast can be swapped out by using a Beast Stables Terminal. Up to three beasts may be stored at a time, depending on perks.
  • 29 creatures can be tamed and trained across all available planets.
  • The Bleed status effect now deals more damage based on PER.
  • Beasts now have their own XP system instead of skills, ranging from levels 1-50.
  • Treatment Kit now cures Disease.
  • Fixed the description of Weapon Focus - Throwing Weapons II.
  • The Zen Marksmanship perk has been added.
  • The DM commands /getscale and /setscale have been added.
  • New station placeables and other models have been added for DM use.
  • Fixed a display issue where quests referenced Gimpassa instead of Gimpassa Hatchling.
  • Slam's recast time has been corrected to 30 seconds per the design document.
  • Recipe items are no longer destroyed if all recipes have already been learned by the user.
  • Infusion now grants First Aid XP when used during combat.
