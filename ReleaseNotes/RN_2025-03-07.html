General Changes

  • Player corvette models have been resized.
  • The Revanite Base has been reworked.
  • New Fabrication recipes have been added.
  • New quests have been added.
  • Korriban areas received quality-of-life improvements, quest updates, and snow world prefabs.
  • Craftable droids T1-T4 have had their crafting costs reduced.
  • All players gain 1 free rebuild token.

Space Updates

  • Bulwark and Repair Field no longer loop to provide extra healing. Visual timers were also fixed.
  • Player corvette models have been resized.
  • Assault Concussion Missiles no longer one-shot targets.
  • All Capital Ship Modules now have their own category in the Engineering Bench.
  • Targeting Array no longer gives a damage boost. It now provides damage instead of evasion decrease.
  • Advanced Thrusters received a description edit with no value changes.
  • Reinforced Plating no longer gives evasion decrease, but does decrease shields.
  • Quad Lasers had oversized fire range adjusted and capacitor costs increased.
  • Turbolasers now deal increased damage from Module Bonus.
  • Laser Cannon Battery had range and damage adjusted, with a slight NPC damage increase.
  • Beam Cannon and Laser Cannon ranges and capacitor costs were adjusted.
  • Bulwark and Repair Field now heal larger amounts to account for single-use fire mechanics.
  • Missile weapons had their minimum shield value adjusted to 10.
  • Capital ship hull and shield scaling increased overall.
  • Warship no longer gains a damage bonus. It has slightly increased armor values, a health boost, and an accuracy increase.
  • Logistics gained a small shield increase and armor increase.
  • Skirmisher lost accuracy increase and gained damage increase and a larger shield pool.
  • Weapons Computer has been added, increasing damage at the cost of lower accuracy.
  • Power Diverter has been added, lowering capacitor for faster shield recharge.
  • E-War Module has been added and generates enmity.
