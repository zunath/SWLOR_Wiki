Changes

  • Fixed a pathing issue with one of the Dathomir areas.
  • Status effects, including auras, now send messages at a range of 20 meters, up from 10. This ensures aura application messages are visible with a maxed-out Shout Range perk.
  • Corrected an issue where respawns were shorter than expected under certain scenarios.
  • Knockdown's duration has been reduced.
  • Reduced the weight of many miscellaneous items.
  • Adjustments have been made to bosses on Dathomir.
