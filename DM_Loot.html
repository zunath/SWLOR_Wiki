DM Items

Introduction

In the dynamic universe of SWLOR (Star Wars Legends of the Old Republic), players can request custom items from a Dungeon Master (DM) or Quest Master (QM). These bespoke pieces, personalized to each character, often rank among the game's most powerful assets. Notably, top-tier weapons and armor, termed as 'Tier 5.5 items', are examples of such powerful, custom creations.

Beyond weaponry and armor, the range of custom items also extends to 'flavor items' that enhance narrative depth and non-equippable spell-casting items. These unique items can imitate mundane powers and offer valuable character buffs, adding an extra layer to gameplay.

These custom items, brought to life by DMs or QMs, intertwine the players' creativity with their character's narrative arc. They serve as powerful in-game tools and symbols of each character's unique journey in the SWLOR universe.

Request Process

Ticket

The process of applying for a custom DM can be done at anytime, though it is at the DM's discretion to grant such an item. Items can be applied for on the #Ticket channel of the SWLOR Discord. 

Response Time

The SWLOR Team is a small, but dedicated team. It often takes time to get to each ticket as there are multiple coming in per hour.
