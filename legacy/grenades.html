Grenade Throwing and Grenade Range

Grenade accuracy-

The user rolls a d100 against a variable DC calculated by these factors; Throwing Skill and Grenade Proficiency. 

100 - 5 - Throwing Skill = DC. A D100 is rolled without any modifiers and combats the prior calculated DC. 

EX: You are throwing a grenade. You have 67 levels in Throwing, and have 6 levels of the Grenade Proficiency Perk. Your DC is 100 - 5 - 67 - 1 = 27. You now roll a D100, and you must beat 27 on the dice to complete the throw. If you had no levels in the Grenade Proficiency Perk, it would be 100 - 5 - 67 = 28

Your range on grenades is based on your strength modifier (not score). The distance unit is based off of the 10x10 meter-tile system of NWN, and as such, no real caliber for distance as a unit exists in either feet or meters. For simplicity, it's easiest to proceed and assume the measurement is being made in meters. 

 10m + (2* Strength Modifier)  = Maximum Throw Distance

EX: Someone with a strength score of 70  will have a modifier of 30, which comes out to 10 meters + 60 meters, or 70 meters. This is roughly equivalent to being able to clear 7 tiles worth of distance with a grenade toss.

 

Grenades and their Functionalities

Fragmentation Grenade: [Grenade Proficiency Level]d6 Fire + DMG bonus, [Grenade Proficiency Level]d6 + DMG bonus, 33% to apply Bleed or Burning, both of which seem to last [Grenade Proficiency Level]+1 ticks before rerolling application (Bleed is 5 magic, Burn is 1 fire).

 

Concussion Grenade: [Grenade Proficiency Level]d12 + DMG bonus Sonic, 33% chance to deafen and knock down affected individuals for [Grenade Proficiency Level]+1 Rounds? Seconds?

 

Flashbang: Deafens for 1d4+1 rounds, 33% chance to Blind for same duration as Deafen

 

Ion: [Grenade Proficiency Level]d6 + DMG bonus Electrical, If ***ONE*** target in the blast is a robot, it will apply a stun effect to everything within the radius for 1d4+1 rounds. If ***ONE*** target in the blast is a Cyborg, there is a 33% chance it will apply a stun to everything within the radius for 1d4+1 rounds

 

Kolto Grenades (NOT BOMBS): [Grenade Proficiency Level] + 1 + Medicine Gear Bonus Healing, lasts for [Grenade Proficiency Level] + 1 Rounds

 

Adhesive: Apply Slow [for rounds = Grenade Prof Level (? unsure)], 33% chance to apply Cutscene Immobilization (Root)

 

Smoke: Apply invisibility while within the radius to all creatures, [Grenade Proficiency Level] + 1 Rounds

 

Kolto Bombs (NOT GRENADES): [Grenade Proficiency Level]*2 + 1 Healing (IS NOT AFFECTED BY MEDICINE BONUS), lasts for [Grenade Proficiency Level] Rounds+1

 

Incendiary: [Grenade Proficiency Level] + 1 + [Grenade Proficiency Level]d6 Fire Damage, lasts for 1d6+1 Rounds

 

Gas: [Grenade Proficiency Level] + 1 + [Grenade Proficiency Level]d6 Acid Damage, lasts for 1d6+1 Rounds
