Welcome to STAR WARS: LEGENDS OF THE OLD REPUBLIC

SWLOR is an RP-Focused server with limited questing, set a year after the Jedi Civil War. Darth Malak is gone, but peace is not so easy to achieve. The Republic and its Jedi protectors work tirelessly to reunite a shattered galaxy, whilst the Sith Empire, now turned Sith Remnant, scramble to hold onto what power they still have. 

An uneasy cease-fire has been negotiated, leading to a Cold War between old adversaries. In the wake of this, a beast stirs within the undercities of gangs and underworld kingpins, eager to take a slice of the galaxy for themselves. Paramilitary groups are on the rise, sensing a demand for their services. It is a time of mercenaries, scoundrels, noble heroes and sinister villains. 

What will your Legend be?
