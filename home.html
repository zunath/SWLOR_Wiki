Welcome to STAR WARS: LEGENDS OF THE OLD REPUBLIC

SWLOR is an RP-focused server with limited questing, set during the Jedi Civil War. Darth Revan leads his followers to war against the Republic and its Jedi protectors. It is a time of mercenaries, scoundrels, noble heroes, and sinister villains. What will your legend be?

This wiki contains all the information you need to get started, helpful guides, and detailed information on a wide variety of topics. Click one of the links below or use the search bar to find an article.
