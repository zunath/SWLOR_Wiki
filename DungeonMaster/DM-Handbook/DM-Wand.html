DM Wand

The Dungeon Master Wand has various tools for DM Client users to edit the world space, NPCs, and PCs. The DM Wand has several options when used:
1. Player: Change Alignment - not used.
2. Player: Macro Inventory Functions:
     1. Take all items
     2. Take all non-equipped items
     3. Identify all items for player
     4. Take all "Uber" items - do not use.
     5. Change Target to next Party member
3. Player: General/Server Functions
     1. Full Player Report - displays a readout of the player to include the following as well as an inventory readout below it:

     2. Toggle Penguin Status - turns the player into a penguin; the player can still use powers.
     3. Send player to jail - sends the player to the jail module located in the Custom Encounter Region
     4. Join Party - does not work.
     5. Remove from Party - does not work.
     6. Map out the Area for the player
     7. Show area map to the target's party
     8. Export all players - do not use.
     9. SAFETY SPACE  - NO FUNCTION
     10. Boot Player
     11. Reload Module
4. Player Store and Recall Copy - do not use.
5. Work with an Object
     1. Destroy Object (target or nearest) - if used on self, will attempt to destroy nearest object similarly to DMFI Destroy Widget.
     2. Turn off Object (target or nearest) - same as above, but will attempt to turn off the object; rarely works.
     3. Turn on the Targeted Object - Opposite of #2. 
     4. Set Facing Due North
     5. Set Facing Due East
     6. Rotate by 30째 (counterclockwise)
     7. Rotate by 45째 (counterclockwise)
     8. Rotate by 90째 (counterclockwise)
     9. Rotate by 180째 (turn halfway around)
6. Change Time of Day/Weather
     1. 1 hour
     2. 4 hours
     3. 8 hours
     4. 24 hours
     5. Make it day
     6. Make it night
     7. Make it clear
     8. Make it rain
     9. Make it snow I'm mildly
     10. Restore default weather
7. Tileset Effects (STEP 1) - this chooses the Z axis for fill effects such as water, lava, and ice. The location will relative to where you target with the cursor after selecting the command.
8. Tileset Effects (fill and flood effects)
     1. Fill a pit or Chasm with water
     2. Fill a pit or Chasm with ice
     3. Fill a pit or Chasm with lava
     4. Fill a pit or Chasm with a sewer effect
     5. Flood a region with water
     6. Flood an area with ice
     7. Flood in area with lava
     8. Flood in area with sewer
     9. Reset to original
9. Tileset Effects 2 (Ground Cover)
     1. Cover the ground with ice
     2. Ground cover effect: grass
     3. Ground cover effect: cave floor
     4. Reset to original
10. Item: Work with an item (must have targeted item directly)
     1. Display item information - targeting an item displays the following info:

     2. Destroy the item
     3. Deplete all charges
     4. Restore to maximum charges
     5. Toggle droppable/undroppable
     6. Toggle cursed flag
     7. Toggle plot flag
     8. Toggle stolen flag

It should be noted that much of utility of this wand is lost on SWLOR's DM Client. The DM Wand is still a valuable tool, however.
