DM Broadcasting and DM Broadcasting as a Player

A DM Client user can send several types of messages to players on the server in a variety of different ways:
/s [message] - a message sent by a DM server-wide and viewable to all players.

/bc [message] - a message that functions identically as a standard shout, but is also sent to #dm-broadcasts on the SWLOR Discord server.
