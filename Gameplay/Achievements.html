Achievements

In SWLOR, achievements are tracked on your account (CD key), not per character. Any character on your account can unlock an achievement; once unlocked, it stays unlocked for the account. Progress toward numeric milestones (kills, crafts, etc.) is also stored on the account. DMs do not receive achievement grants.

Viewing achievements

Open your character sheet and use the achievements control to open the Achievements window. The list is paginated (25 entries per page). Unlocked achievements appear in green; locked ones in red. Select a row to see its full description and, if you have it, the acquired timestamp.

Pop-up notifications

When you earn a new achievement, you may get a small Achievement Unlocked banner and sound. You can turn this off in your character settings (Display achievement notification); the achievement is still granted and still appears in the achievements list.

How achievements unlock

  • Combat: Lifetime enemy kills credited to your account when you land killing blows (tiers at 10, 50, 500, 2,000, 10,000, and 100,000 kills).
  • Perks: Count increases when you buy perks (1, 20, 50, 100, 500 perks learned).
  • Skill points: Count increases when you gain skill points (1, 50, 150, 250, 500, 1000).
  • Quests: Count increases when you complete quests (1, 10, 50, 100, 500, 1000, 1500, 2000, 3500, 5000).
  • Crafting: Count increases on successful crafts (1, 10, 50, 100, 500, 1000, 1500, 2000, 3500, 5000).
  • Exploration: Entering certain areas grants the matching achievement once (areas are configured with an exploration achievement id).
  • Specific quests: For example, completing The Legendary Rod grants its achievement.

Duplicate grants are ignored: if you already have an achievement, nothing changes.
