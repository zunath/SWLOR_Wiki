Utility skills

Utility skills aid in healing, buffs, electronics, and piloting.

Devices XP comes from dealing damage or healing in combat. Leadership buffs can be active during combat or gathering; the Dedication perk can also feed Leadership XP from most sources (not while piloting). Piloting gains fastest from high-tier mining in space; see the Piloting guide for detail.

Skill details (from server)

First Aid

Ability to treat bodily injuries in the field with healing kits and stim packs.

  • Max ranks: 50
  • Contributes to the character skill cap

Leadership

Ability to handle people, negotiate, and manage relations.

  • Max ranks: 50
  • Contributes to the character skill cap

Piloting

Ability to pilot starships, follow navigation charts, and control starship systems.

  • Max ranks: 50
  • Contributes to the character skill cap
