Utility Skills and Perks Overview

Utility skills are a series of skills that aid in various situations. There are some overlap with combat skills, such as Devices, but these skills and most perks associated largely focus on healing or helping allies rather than dealing direct damage.

Leveling these skills can be done fairly passively during combat and require little effort to do so more than simple time. Devices cannot miss, so as long as damage is dealt by a Device skill or healing is applied during a fight; experience will be gained when the creature is killed by the player or a party member. Leadership buffs simply need to be active during combat or gathering. Leadership is the only skill that can power level itself. The Dedication perk is fantastic for leveling every skill except piloting and even without investment into Leadership or the Social stat the first tier is a great choice for new players that can be refunded when their build is complete.

Piloting skill can be increased by ship combat, but the most effective method is to mine the highest tier nodes you can safely mine. Mining grants significantly more experience for piloting than space combat does. For a more detailed breakdown of Piloting you can find Ragnar's Piloting guide here!
