Masteries

Masteries are DM granted ranks in a skill that go above and beyond the mechanical abilities of the game. These allow you to customize your character and specialize in things to a greater degree than the game normally allows. Masteries can be applied once a character is two weeks old and take 28 days per rank of Mastery, excluding rank 5's which take an extended amount of time. A character may only have a single Rank 5 Mastery as their specialty, no other Masteries may exceed Rank 4.

Rarities:
None - Free to take, no restriction

Rare - DM team wishes to restrict these. Handed out on case by case basis.

Off Limits - Included only to state that they are not allowed, do not request them.

 

Just because a Mastery lists a description that involves in-game skills does not mean it has an actual in-game benefit. Masteries should not be chosen for in-game benefits, but for roleplay. 

There is no guarantee your chosen Mastery will have a token or in-game benefit.

 

You may apply for Masteries not on this list, as well, that are more RP focused. (Eg. Breakdancing, drawing, singing)
