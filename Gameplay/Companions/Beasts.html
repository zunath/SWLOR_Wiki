Beasts Overview

Beasts are NPC companions that can be tamed and summoned via the Beast Mastery skills. Beast mastery perks can be found here!

Catch rate is determined by the following formula.  Catch Rate = 40 + (skill - Creature Level) * 3 + social modifier * 4;
Catch rates cannot exceed 95, but can be 0. Your modifier, like normal NWN, is 0 at 10 and increases by 1 for every 2 stat point increases above 10.
