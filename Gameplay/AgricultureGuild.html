The Agriculture Guild in Veles is separate from the Engineering, Smithery, and Fabrication guilds by the Market Terminals. Instead, find it in the restaurant Tapani Delights next to Racing Jim's Cantina—the guildmaster is in the back, in the kitchen. Agriculture guildmasters also appear elsewhere in the galaxy, often near starports.

How guild progression works

All SWLOR guilds share the same core rules. Completing a task pays credits and Guild Points (GP). GP is increased by your current guild rank, the Guild Relations perk (Leadership), and your character’s Social ability modifier. When your GP crosses the next threshold, your guild rank rises. Maximum rank is 5; GP stops accumulating past the cap for that system.

GP is cumulative (it is not reset when you rank up). Total GP milestones: 1,000 for rank 1, 5,000 for rank 2, 15,000 for rank 3, 30,000 for rank 4, and 45,000 for rank 5. You must be at least guild rank 1 before the guildmaster will open the guild shop (rank 0 members can still take tasks).

Tasks are organized into five tiers. In dialogue, each task is labeled with a bracketed tier (e.g. [Rank 1] through [Rank 5]). That number is one higher than the internal minimum guild rank: tier 1 tasks need guild rank 0, tier 2 need rank 1, … tier 5 need rank 4. You only see tasks your rank qualifies for.

For each tier, the guild can only have up to 10 different tasks active at once if the tier has more than 10 possible tasks; which tasks are active is chosen when guild tasks are initialized on the server.

Guild Points from a task use the table in the Engineering Guild page (base GP per tier). The GP you actually earn is increased by your current guild rank, Guild Relations, and Social, as described above.

Credits use the same per-tier base amounts (1,000 through 7,200). The payout adds 5% of that base per point of Social ability modifier and 5% of the base per rank of Guild Relations; guild rank does not change credit rewards.

Agriculture Guild Tasks

Tasks are repeatable item turn-ins: gather the listed food or ingredient stacks and use Give Report on the guildmaster. Names and order match AgricultureGuildQuestDefinition.cs (in-game display uses the same item names).

 

Agriculture Shop Items

After you reach guild rank 1, choose Show me the guild shop. The dialogue lists Rank 1 through Rank 5 store buttons matching your current guild rank (the same ranks as in the main guild header). Ranks 2–5 repeat some staples (for example bags) with new gear unlocked at each tier.
