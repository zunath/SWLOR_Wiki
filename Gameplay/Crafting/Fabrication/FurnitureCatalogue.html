Here exists a visual and crafting requirement reference for all placeable decoration items presently accessible through the Fabrication Crafting system.

Use the section navigation pane to the left to quickly navigate between furniture functions and types.

 

Each cell contains various information regarding the placeable, including its given name, item level relative to enhancement and crafting skill, number of enhancement slots, and raw materials required for Fabricator creation.

Slots are indicated with filled or empty circles.

○○ = No modification slots available

●○ = One modification slot available

●● = Two modification slots available

 

Special notes or existing/known issues will also be shown as red subtext.
