Smithery Overview

Smithing or Smithery is one of four crafting skills in SWLOR. Smithery is used to craft weapons and non-droid armor. Skill ranks, perks, and crafting gear determine how well you craft; core attributes do not.

Smithery requires an investment of 88 Skill Points to purchase every skill within the tree, though most people would recommend only purchasing the smithing actions Basic Touch, Master's Mend, and Waste Not, bringing the total required for all recipes and must-have actions down to 81 SP.

All recipes not yet learned that are listed on this wiki (with the exception of training sabers, which are granted by DM's to appropriate players) are earned by defeating difficult miniboss enemies on Tatooine and Dathomir such as Chirodactyls, Rancors, Dark Side Adepts, Bounty Hunters, and Krayt Dragons.
