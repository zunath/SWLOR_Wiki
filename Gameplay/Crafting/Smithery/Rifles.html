Rifle Overview

Skill: Ranged
Accuracy Stat: Agility
Damage Stat: Perception

Rifles make for excellent ranged weapons that deal impressive damage. While they may lack the number of attacks the pistol can produce in short succession, they hit much, much harder. They will fall shorter in DPS against most targets, but they excel against targets with higher Damage Resistance compared to other ranged weapons, allowing you to punch through those defenses and still deal reliable damage. They also make for exceptional utility with the ability to tranquilize an enemy for up to 12 seconds as long as they don't take damage, or inflicting blind from a safe distance with their weapon skill Perks.
