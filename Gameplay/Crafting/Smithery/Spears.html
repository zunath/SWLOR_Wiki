Spear Overview

Skill: Two-Handed
Accuracy Stat: Perception
Damage Stat: Might

Spears, or polearms, are the key to some of the biggest numbers in SWLOR. They offer the same base damage as Heavy Vibroblades, but instead of more common crits Spears deal 3x damage rather than 2x damage on crits leaving your enemies really feeling it when you land them. Spears also bring some of the rarest utility to the battlefield, offering a weapon skill that can interrupt targets and break concentration. While this may not have as many uses as other utility skills, it can have a big impact when it does.
