Shuriken Overview

Skill: Ranged
Accuracy Stat: Agility
Damage Stat: Might

Shuriken, or thrown weapons, are excellent ranged for Force Sensitives, in particular. An excellent weapon to pair alongside the shuriken is the twinblade. With their reliance on similar stats and the ability for Force Users to buff both their accuracy and damage stat, these make for an ideal weapon, allowing you to keep your distance and deal reliable damage. Though, these weapons are also useful for Standards, with weapon skills that deal an AoE damage and inflict bleed from a distance, as well as allowing you to invest in your Agility to increase stamina and survivability, unlike most Might based weapons.
