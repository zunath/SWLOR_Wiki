Katar Overview

Skill: Martial Arts
Accuracy Stat: Agility
Damage Stat: Perception

Katars are bladed fist-style weapons used in Martial Arts. Their weapon skills allow them to apply Shock and Poison statuses to enemies, making them very useful. Katars can be dual wielded with the one-handed Dual Wield one-handed Perk, making them even more effective.
