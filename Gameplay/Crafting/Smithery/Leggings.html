Legging Overview

Leggings are heavy armor that occupies the foot slot. Leggings offer high defense and HP compared to their Boot counterparts, but at the expense of less diversity and lower stamina and FP as well as lower regen of respective pools. A character, especially a Standard (someone without Force sensitivity), in heavy armor may wish to invest into Agility to counteract the loss of stamina and stamina regen or mix and match heavy and light armor.

Heavy armor (excluding general armors) double your casting time for all Force abilities.
