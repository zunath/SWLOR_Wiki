Electroblade/Lightsaber Overview

Skill: One-handed
Accuracy Stat: Agility
Damage Stat: Perception

Electroblades are extremely useful and reliable weapons. Though useable by non-Force Sensitives in the world of Star Wars, they substitute here for the Lightsaber stand-in to allow you to perform all of the same abilities and actions as your glowstick wielding compatriots. They offer excellent combat mobility with the ability to leap to nearby enemies using the Force Leap weapon skill Perk as well as a decent, though not often used, weapon skill that can reduce the enemy's evasion, giving it decent utility as well.

For those who prefer it, you can also take Strong Style to change your accuracy stat to Perception and your damage stat to Might. You may deal slightly more damage than your AGI colleagues but most find the extreme drop in defense not worth it, unless pursuing crafts like Smithing that require greater Might, or as excellent flavor for a Djem So focused character.
