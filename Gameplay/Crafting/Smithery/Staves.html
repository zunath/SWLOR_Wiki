Staff Overview

Accuracy Stat: Perception
Damage Stat: Might

Staves are Martial Arts weapons that occupy both hands. There are two prominent styles that can be made use of for Staves via their respective Perks. Crushing style greatly increases the damage you deal with each hit in proportion to your MGT and allow you to crit more often while Flurry Style allows you to use Agility for accuracy and Perception for damage, as well as giving you an extra attack per round. It also offers excellent utility by allowing you to inflict Blind or use the Legsweep Perk for an additional Knockdown attack.
