Twinblade Overview

Skill: Two-Handed
Accuracy Skill: Agility
Damage Skill: Might

Twinblades, double bladed sword staves, are excellent weapons for Force users, in particular. They offer he safety and survivability of Agility based weapons while also allowing you to buff both your accuracy and damage stats. These pair excellently with Shuriken thanks to their identical stat reliance. Twinblades deal decent damage while allowing a decent stamina pool and the many other boons Agility allows that most Might based weapons cannot benefit from. Standards may also find use in this weapon, with a weapon skill that inflicts an evasion debuff on opponents as well as granting an AoE attack.
