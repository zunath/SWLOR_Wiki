The Engineering Guild buys starship modules and ship deeds you deliver. In Veles, the guildmaster stands with the Smithery and Fabrication guildmasters at the Market Terminals. The Agriculture Guild is separate (Tapani Delights kitchen). Other worlds may have their own guildmaster placements.

How guild progression works

See Agriculture Guild for Guild Points, cumulative rank thresholds, rotating tasks (up to 10 per tier), shop access at guild rank 1+, and how dialog [Rank 1–5] maps to minimum internal ranks 0–4.

Base task rewards (before bonuses)

Guild Points: The base GP values above are increased by your current guild rank, Guild Relations, and your Social ability modifier when the task pays out (see Agriculture Guild).

Credits: The base credit values are increased by Social (5% of the base per ability modifier point) and Guild Relations (5% of the base per perk rank). Your guild rank does not change credit rewards (Quest.CalculateQuestGoldReward).

Engineering Guild tasks

Repeatable turn-ins: craft or acquire the item, then Give Report. Wording matches the quest title (amount x name). Only a subset of each tier’s tasks is offered at once when the tier has more than ten definitions.

 

Engineering Guild shop

After guild rank 1+, open Show me the guild shop and pick Rank 1–5 to match the vendor tier. Items below are parsed from the module merchants gp_eng_1 … gp_eng_5.
