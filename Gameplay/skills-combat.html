Combat skills

Combat skills are various skills that deal damage or offer other combat effects, such as Force powers, armor for gear, and Beast Mastery for pets.

The method to level most combat skills requires using them in real fights: land at least one hit (where applicable) and have you or a party member get the killing blow. Force powers and Devices cannot miss. Armor levels passively while worn in combat. Beast pets gain XP when summoned; they need not attack.

Skill details

Armor

Ability to effectively wear and defend against attacks with armor.

  • Max ranks: 50
  • Contributes to the character skill cap

Beast Mastery

Ability to tame wild animals, raise them, and train them.

  • Max ranks: 50
  • Contributes to the character skill cap

Devices

Ability to use grenades, bombs, and other electronics.

  • Max ranks: 50
  • Contributes to the character skill cap
