Overview

Mon Cala, also known as Mon Calamari or Dac, is an oceanic planet in the Mon Calamari system—a binary star system in the Calamari sector of the Outer Rim and the Slice. A single moon orbits the world.

Legends & society

The oceans of Mon Cala teem with life, including the two major sentient species—the Mon Calamari and the Quarren. Both peoples typically build cities underwater, though surface and floating settlements exist. Traditionally the planet fell under a single monarch; despite political friction, the two species have often maintained mutual respect.

Coral reefs shelter countless fish and crustaceans. Reefs thrive in the light of Mon Cala’s twin suns; deeper trenches favor bioluminescent flora.

Sparse islands form archipelagos—rocky cliffs, sandy beaches, and jungle interiors. Polar seas hold ice floes. Many expatriates settle offworld (for example the moon Trask) to escape court politics.

Mon Cala is famous for shipbuilding: the Mon Calamari Shipyards and the Quarren-led Free Dac Volunteers Engineering Corps produced distinctive hulls for civilian and military use alike.

In SWLOR (gameplay & world)

Mon Cala is a full adventuring planet. Dock at Space - Mon Cala Orbit with a registered starship and land at the colony or player holdings marked on the chart (see the Piloting Guide).

Coral Isles (inner & outer) — reef and island exploration with native predators such as vipers, aradiles, and amphihydrus-class creatures. Gathering tables here include scordspar and plagionite veins, ancient and oak-like timber, mid-tier herbs and vegetables, and upgraded fiber bushes—good mid-level crafting fodder.

Dac City (surface) — urban waterfront fishing with a city-surface table (mixed common and mid-tier saltwater catches, including jellyfish-family and reef prize fish).

Sharptooth Jungle — southern jungle waters and cave systems host nocturnal and daytime catches; cave fisheries include shellfish, blindfish, and predatory sport fish. Land spawns include microtench-line creatures in cavern loops.

Sunkenhead swamps — marsh and bayou-style districts with octotenchs, scorchies, and microtench swarms; separate fishing tables for crayfish, frogs, and coastal bait fish.

Eco-terrorist storyline pockets — organized enemy packs and a leader-tier spawn support higher-risk outdoor combat away from the reefs.

Gathering snapshot — Mon Cala surface tables emphasize plagionite alongside scordspar, rich herb and vegetable patches, fiber, and heavy ancient-tree logging in the isles.
