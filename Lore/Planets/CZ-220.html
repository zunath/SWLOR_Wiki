Overview

CZ-220 is SWLOR’s new-player hub and tutorial facility: a compact station where characters finalize appearance, learn core systems, and pick up quests before heading dirtside. Fictionally it sits on the Viscara approach corridor—the shuttle ride in character creation is headed here, and Republic logistics (including dockyard access) treat CZ-220 as a resupply node on the Mytaranor frontier (see The Republic wiki article).

Layout

Player-facing areas use clear labels:

  • CZ-220 - Hangar — first footsteps after the intro shuttle; NPCs orient you toward the main complex.
  • CZ-220 - Offices & Labs — reception, quest givers, and station operations (including Selan Flembek, the receptionist who issues the hub quest chain).
  • CZ-220 - Maintenance Level — lower-deck hazards, scavenging, crafting-tutorial resources, and security trouble (see quests involving Harlon Linth).

Key NPCs & quests

The flagship introduction arc is Selan's Request: complete three station tasks and return receipts to Selan for XP, credits, and—critically—the CZ-220 shuttle pass key item. That pass is required for normal use of ship management at starports—without it, terminals will turn you away unless staff grant an exception.

Other station contacts include:

  • Avix Tatham — mining coordinator; ore collection and refinery trainee beats.
  • Halron Linth — security; maintenance-level incidents, mynock culls, droid malfunctions, and the Colicoid experiment line (journal text in-game may alternate spelling; it is one officer).
  • Crafting Terminal Droid operator — crafting tutorials (e.g. smithery supply turn-ins referencing a Basic Knife).

Additional CZ-220 content includes mynock culls, junk pile and supply cache loot tables, Colicoid Experiment boss-tier content, and Veldite mining spawns—check your journal after talking to station staff.

Account milestone

Completing Selan's Request also marks your account as having finished the new-player tutorial on the OOC progression track. IC, you still decide how your character feels about corporate-run orientation.
