Outer Rim Territories (frontier classification)

System

Hutlar system (SWLOR setting)

Trade routes

Off major lanes; traffic is expeditionary—mining consortiums, Mandalorian cohorts, xenobiologists, and black-market salvage teams.

Rotation period

26 standard hours

Orbital period

412 local days (approximate)

Physical information

Atmosphere

Type I at habitable elevations; high tundra and cave systems may require cold-weather gear for long exposure.

Climate

Polar and subarctic: ice storms, whiteouts, and brief thaw windows that turn valleys into mud traps.

Primary terrain

Primary: Tundra steppe and wind-scoured valleys
Secondary: Box canyons, frozen rivers, and karst caves
Tertiary: Engineered hive complexes burrowed under the ice line

Points of interest (broad)

Qion-region settlements and outposts; Mandalorian fortifications; abandoned corporate test sites; and xeno hives that treat surface dwellers as seasonal prey.

Societal information

Population

Sparse humans and near-humans at the Outpost; Mandalorian war camps; scientific and military detachments; smugglers using thaw caves as layover berths.

Languages

Galactic Basic; Mando'a in Ka'ra-adjacent quarters; hive clicks and pheromone trade cant in the tunnels (RP flavor).

Government & affiliation

No unified world government—strength is local. Clan banners, corporate charters, and “who holds the pass” matter more than senate districts. Expect tension between outsiders and anyone who claims the Qion by blood or beskar.

Economy

Exotic organics, geothermal power experiments, mercenary contracts, and salvage from forgotten wars frozen into the permafrost.

History (setting tone)

Hutlar is the sort of world cartographers file under “hostile ecology, marginal yield.” That profile attracts exactly the wrong kind of pioneers: the ambitious, the exiled, and the Mandalorian. The Jedi Civil War’s aftermath did not warm the ice; it only scattered more wreckage to strip and more secrets to bury.

In SWLOR (gameplay & world)

Hutlar is a full destination with Space - Hutlar Orbit. Surface zones use the prefix Hutlar - ….

Qion region is the heart of the planet’s exploration loop: Qion Tundra, Qion Valley, Qion Foothills, and the dramatic Qion Box Canyon (including Fort Ka'ra, a Mandalorian stronghold).

Hive complex (dungeon chain): Qion Hive, Approach; Outer Hive; Ziggurat; and Broodmother—escalating difficulty suitable for coordinated groups.

Frozen wastes: Frozen Wastes and Frozen Caves for wilderness survival, hunting, and chase scenes.

Other sites: Abandoned Outpost and Cloning Test Site support horror, sci-fi, and black-ops RP; the main Outpost anchors safer hub roleplay.

Faction links: Mandalorian themes are explicit in Fort Ka'ra. For Republic or Hutt context, treat Hutlar as a cold-storage frontier where paperwork freezes faster than fuel lines—see also The Mandalorians on the wiki.
