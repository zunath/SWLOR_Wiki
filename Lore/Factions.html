To join a faction in the Star Wars: Legends of the Old Republic Discord server, use ‘?rank Faction -’ followed by the exact name of the faction above.  Refer to the Discord Faction Listing for specific examples of all factions.  Players should only join these faction channels if they have a character in the faction or they have a good faith intention to create a character and join that faction.
